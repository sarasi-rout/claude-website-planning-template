🎯 Step 2: How Do You Want to Position Yourself?

Tips for Success

Keep everything in your Claude Project: Save this template, your reflections, and all drafts in a single Claude Project so you have all the context in one place.

Be specific with Claude: The more specific your prompts, the better help you'll get. Include examples, constraints, and context.

Iterate on your positioning: Your first draft positioning statement probably won't be perfect—refine it as you work through your content.

Show, don't just tell: Use your project descriptions to demonstrate your skills and approach rather than just listing them.

Get feedback: Share your positioning and key content with trusted colleagues or mentors before finalizing.
